About the LEARN Meeting

What it is

The annual gathering of Sri Lanka’s research & education community. In 2025 we launch two new Working Groups—GenAI Empowerment and AgriTech—and run a LEARN Services Awareness session.

Who should attend

  • Official delegates of member institutions (AGM)
  • Academics, researchers, IT leaders, administrators (WGs)
  • Campus IT, library, e-learning teams (Services)

Working Groups

Hands-on communities driving projects, training, and real outcomes for 2025–26.

WG 1: AgriTech

IoTRemote SensingAIData Platforms

Purpose: Connect agriculture faculties, research centres, and IT teams to apply digital technologies across education, research, and extension.

Expected Outcomes (2025–26)
  • 2–3 collaborative pilots (e.g., smart farm sensor network; shared agri-data repository)
  • Quarterly knowledge-sharing sessions and a community portal
  • Published roadmap with roles, milestones, and metrics
Who should join:

Academics & researchers in agriculture; IT/IoT specialists; administrators supporting agri projects.

WG 2: GenAI Empowerment

TeachingResearchStudent ServicesOps

Purpose: Build national capacity to use Generative AI responsibly in teaching, research, student services, and NREN operations.

Expected Outcomes (2025–26)
  • 2–3 pilot projects (e.g., student services chatbot; AI-assisted teaching toolkit)
  • 2–3 training programs for faculty, researchers, and IT teams
  • Practical guidelines for safe, ethical GenAI use in higher education
Who should join:

CS/IT academics, learning technologists, librarians, student services leaders, and campus IT managers.

Main Sessions

16th Annual General Meeting (AGM)

Participants: Official delegates of LEARN member organizations (VCs or nominees).

LEARN Board Meeting

Participants: LEARN Board members (Closed meeting).

LEARN Services Awareness Session

A session for the broader LEARN community and prospective members. Topics include:

  • Identity & Access: eduID, identity federation, eduGAIN
  • Connectivity & Mobility: eduroam, eduVPN
  • Cybersecurity & Support: LEARN-CSIRT, TAC
  • Collaboration & Cloud: conferencing, e-learning, storage

Bring your questions! We’ll share deployment guides, contacts, and success stories.

Opening Ceremony & Social Dinner

The Opening Ceremony welcomes the community, introduces the WGs, and features a keynote address. The Social Dinner (Day 1 evening) brings everyone together to build relationships and kickstart collaborations.
